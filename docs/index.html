Yuhuai(Tony) Wu


Co-Founder of xAI.
Previously at Google N2Formal led by Christian Szegedy.
Postdoc at Stanford with Percy Liang and Jay McClelland.
PhD at U of Toronto with Roger Grosse and Jimmy Ba.

Research Interests


My primary research interest is building machines that can reason. I have chosen mathematics as a starting point to study reasoning, with the aim of creating an automated mathematician.
